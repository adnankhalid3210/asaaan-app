Safety Tips!

For Service Providers

So you accept a hiring request and you're going to work - we know you might have some concerns over making a safe journey, so here are some tips for you:

1). Make sure that the person or organization is hiring you for their problems are authenticated user of Asaan.
2). Always check your payment you received after you completed your service. We are not responsible for any type of payment forgery.
3). Don't accept any hiring request for any security risk address.

For Customers

If you are hiring a person or oraization for your problem - we know you might have some concerns over making a safe solution of your problem, so here are some tips for you:

1). Before hiring any individual or organziation, make sure that you have seen all the reviews of that particular service provider.
2). Make sure that the service provider is authenticated by Asaan.
3). Before hiring any service provider, make sure you have visited the complete profile of the service provider.
